About me:


Freelancer photographer, focused on helping my clients to get the best images for their projects. I am 26 years old based in the Netherlands, where I study design of interior and photography at the University of Amsterdam. I have been working with different architets and desiners. My background in design helps me to get the best images from your work. I am creative person who likes to work with different styles of photography, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills. My work is always based on the principle of "Quality over Quantity" and my clients are always satisfied with the results. I am passioned about nature, people and, of course, design. I have worked with architets and interior designers, which resulted beautiful images. Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or you want to know more about my work.
